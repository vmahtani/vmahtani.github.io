NBA Playoff Confidence Pool

Basics :
  1. Rank the 16 NBA playoff teams (From 16 best to 1 worst)
  2. After submitting your rankings, you will receive an email with info on payment and a link to the group scoreboard
  3. Deadline - April 16th 1:00pm
Ready to rank? ->

More Info:
  • Rankings apply to the entire playoffs (rank them one time and you are done, similar to an ncaa bracket)
  • Team Playoff Wins * Your Confidence Ranking for that Team = Total Points
  • Example: Last year's scoreboard -> 2021 Confidence Scoreboard
  • Payouts: TBD based on # entrants.
Sample Strategy:
  • Map out the NBA playoff bracket
  • Your 16 & 15 rankings are your NBA finalists
  • Your 14 & 13 rankings are Conference Finalist losers
  • Your 9 - 12 rankings are 2nd round losers
  • Your 1 - 8 rankings are 1st round losers
  • From there, you can tweak, hedge, etc accordingly
