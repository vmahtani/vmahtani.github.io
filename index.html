NBA Playoff Confidence Pool

Basics :
  1. Rank the 16 NBA playoff teams (From 16 best to 1 worst)
  2. After submitting your rankings, you will receive an email with info on payment and link to group scoreboard
  3. Deadline -- May 22nd 2:15pm (pool locks at start of first game)
  4. Enjoy the games
Ready to rank? ->

More Info:
  • Rankings apply to the entire playoffs (rank them one time and you are done. similar to ncaa bracket)
  • Playoff wins * Confidence Rank = User Points
  • Example: Last year's scoreboard -> 2020 Confidence Scoreboard
Strategy:
  • Map out the NBA playoff bracket
  • Your 16 & 15 rankings are your NBA finalists
  • Your 14 & 13 rankings are Conference Finalist losers
  • Your 9 - 12 rankings are 2nd round losers
  • Your 1 - 8 rankings are 1st round losers
  • From there, you can tweak, hedge, etc accordingly
